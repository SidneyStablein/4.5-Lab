About

My passion for graphic design started in 2017, when I first started to use Adobe Photoshop and Illsutrator for an art assignment in highschool. Since then I have worked as a Designer & Social Media Coordinator on a local newspaper and as an Graphic Design intern at Service Express. Reading “Hooked” and through taking classes for my degree in Business Administration, I became fascinated with psychology in relation to consumer marketing. Both have led me to where I am now, with a new passion for User Experience design and research.
